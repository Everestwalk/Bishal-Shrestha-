Message from the Principal

Education is not just a process of giving knowledge for a future job but a lifelong process which creates an understanding of moral and ethical values to guide one's life in a right path. So, at this institution, we focus on complete development of our students by involving them both towards studies and extracurricular activities. I'm sure this will help our children to realize, understand and explore their talents."If a plant is carefully nurtured by a gardener, it will become good and produce fruits". Therefore, I believe that children must be given good training from their childhood itself. That is what we do at Aadhunik Boarding Secondary School.

Why Aadhunik Boarding School?


Located at Kamalamai Municipality-6, Panityanki Sindhuli, Aadhunik School is a highly regarded English medium co-educational school. It was established in 2064 B.S. It has been regulated under the team of trained academicians, professors, distinguished educators, social workers and entrepreneurs with sole objective of providing world class education. It has become the center of learning for quality education. Aadhunik holds secular and democratic values. We vow to shape students as the need of new millennium providing them quality education. We have been preparing to broaden our horizons. In the 21st century, our endeavors have been directed towards making our student: self reliant, successful and secure.
